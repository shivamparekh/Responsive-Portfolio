About Me

My name is Shivam Parekh. I graduated from the University of Central Florida with a degree in Advertising and Public Relations, with a minor in Sociology. I am currently enrolled in the UCF Coding Boot Camp. Some cool companies I have worked with include: Bloomberg, Amazon, UCF Athletics and Edible Arrangements.

I am currently employed as a Brand Standards Partner at Edible Arrangements. In this role I have been able to learn from and contribute to to several different facets of the company. I have been able to make/cultivate sales, develop marketing plans, and learn to understand operational effeciency. I have worked with and/or helped stores all over the US and parts of Canada. Currently, my territory consists of over 130 locations in the states of Florida, North Carolina, and West Virginia.
